My Journey

I grew up in Nashville, Tennessee, and started playing volleyball around the age of 8. My older sister, Sam, played volleyball so I was constantly exposed to it.

During high school, I played travel volleyball and that is how I got in touch with the Gonzaga volleyball coaches. They reached out to me and I came on a vist during my junior year of high school. It was my first time on the West Coast and I immediately fell in love with the city, the campus, and the people. I commited to Gonzaga shortly after my visit and started planning my move across the country.

Being a student-athlete at the college level requires an extreme amount of commitment. It requires a significant amount of dedication and time management. This expeirence, that I am blessed enough to be a part of, has helped shape who I am today.
